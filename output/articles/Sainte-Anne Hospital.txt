
On [[Chronology|November 18, 1953]], [[Jacques Lacan|Lacan]] held his first public [[seminar]] at [[Sainte-Anne Hospital]] -- soon after the establishment of the ''[[Société Française de Psychanalyse]]'' ([[SFP]]).
