The '''''Encyclopédie française''''' was a [[France|French]] [[wp:encyclopedia|encyclopedia]] conceived by [[wp:Anatole de Monzie|Anatole de Monzie]] and [[wp:Lucien Febvre|Lucien Febvre]]. It appeared between [[{{Y}}|1935]] and [[{{Y}}|1966]].

[[Lacan]]'s [[article on the family]] ([[Les complexes familiaux dans la formation de l'individu]]) is published in the [[Encyclopédie Française]]. 

* [[Jacques Lacan|Lacan, Jacques]]. (1984). [[Les complexes familiaux dans la formation de l'individu]] (pp. 23-30; written for Encyclopédie française, Vol. 8). Paris: Larousse. (Original work published [[{{Y}}|1938]])
