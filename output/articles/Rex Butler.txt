REX BUTLER is a Senior Lecturer in English, Media and Art History at the University of Queensland. He is the author of Jean Baudrillard: The Defense of the Real (Sage) in 1999. Excerpts from his [http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0826469957/nosubject-20/ Slavoj Zizek: Live Theory (Polity Press)] appear in [http://www.lacan.com/zizek-signifier.htm Lacan Dot Com]. In 2005 he edited with Scott Stephens Interrogating the Real the first volume of the collected writings of Slavoj Zizek. The introduction to the second volume The Universal Exception (forthcoming 2006), [http://www.lacan.com/zizway.htm Slavoj Zizek's Third Way] appears in Lacan Dot Com.
He co-wrote with Scott Stephens ''[http://www.lacan.com//newspaper7.htm Play Fuckin' Loud: Zizek vs. the Left]'' which appears in The Symptom, Issue 7, and in lacanian ink 28 [http://www.lacan.com/lacinkXXVIII7.htm The Sound of Silence: Wagner with Stalin].
