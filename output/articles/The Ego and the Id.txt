The Ego and the Id was written by [[Sigmund Freud]] in 1923.

The contents of the book are the following

I.   Consciousness and what is Uncounsciousness

II.  The [[Ego]] and the Id

III. The Ego and the Super-Ego (Ego Ideal)

IV.  The two classes of Instinct

V.   The dependent relationships of the Ego
