Three [[children]] were [[born]] from this [[marriage]], [[Caroline]] in [[{{Y}}#1934||1934]], [[Thibaut]] in [[{{Y}}#1939|1939]] and [[Sibylle]] in [[{{Y}}#1940|1940]].
