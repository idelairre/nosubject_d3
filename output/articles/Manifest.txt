Manifest content is the narrative that the dreamer tells about his or her dream. In contemporary usage, the criterion <i>manifest</i> is also applied to other types of verbal production and to behaviors. Sigmund Freud contrasted manifest content to the latent dream thoughts brought out by psychoanalytic interpretation.</p>
